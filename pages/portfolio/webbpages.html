Bhromaon

Assignment in the course IT:Tech for women. The assignment was to recreate a responsive design of the fictive company Bhromaon handed to us in Figma. The assignment did not include functionallity - links/buttons/form do not work.

In this project we had a timeframe, we had to complete the project in 12 hours. I managed to finnish in time, other than a few details that I've fixed later

It was a good lesson to have to plan your work to be able to make it. I started with the basic structure in doing all the sections and major styling, and then took every section to fix the details.
